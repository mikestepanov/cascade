# tl;dv - Competitor Analysis

> **Category:** End-User Meeting Recorder
> **Type:** Proprietary SaaS (Freemium focus)
> **Website:** https://tldv.io

---

## Overview

**tl;dv** (Too Long; Didn't View) is a meeting recorder for Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams that aggressively targets the "freemium" market with generous free tiers.

## Platforms

- Google Meet
- Zoom
- Microsoft Teams

## Pricing

- **Free:** Unlimited recordings, limited AI summaries.
- **Pro:** ~$20/user/mo (Full AI features, integrations).
- **Enterprise:** Custom.

## Features

- **Recording:** High-quality video recording.
- **Timestamps:** Manual or AI-generated bookmarks during calls.
- **Clips:** Easy video clipping to share moments ("Reels" for meetings).
- **AI:** Summaries and action items.

## Strengths ✅

- **Growth Strategy:** Viral loop through free recordings + watermarks.
- **UX:** Very simple, "fun" brand.
- **Generous Free Tier:** "Unlimited recordings" is a huge draw.

## Weaknesses ❌

- **Transcription Quality:** Often rated lower than Otter/Fireflies/Read.
- **PM Integrations:** primarily creates clips/links, doesn't deeply sync "Action Items" as "Issues" in Jira/Linear seamlessly.

## Nixelo Strategy

Beat them on **integration**. tl;dv is a "sidecar" app. Nixelo's meeting intelligence is _native_ to where the work happens.
