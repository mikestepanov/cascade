# Gong - Competitor Analysis

> **Category:** Revenue Intelligence (Sales)
> **Type:** Enterprise SaaS
> **Website:** https://gong.io

---

## Overview

**Gong** captures customer interactions across phone, web conferencing, and email to deliver insights for sales teams. It is the gold standard for "Conversation Intelligence."

## Pricing

- **Enterprise Only:** Typically ~$1,200 - $1,600 /user/year + Platform fees.
- Not for individuals or small teams.

## Features

- **Deal Intelligence:** Analyzes pipeline risk based on conversations.
- **Coaching:** AI feedback on sales reps' talk-to-listen ratios.
- **Market Intelligence:** Tracks competitor mentions across all calls.
- **Deep Analytics:** Keywords, topics, sentiment analysis.

## Strengths ✅

- **Depth:** The analytics are incredible for sales leaders.
- **Category Creator:** They defined this market.
- **Accuracy:** Very high quality transcription and diarization.

## Weaknesses ❌

- **Cost:** Prohibitively expensive for non-sales use cases.
- **Focus:** useless for Engineering standups or Product planning.

## Nixelo Strategy

**Gong is not a direct competitor.**
However, Gong proves that **data inside meetings is valuable**. Nixelo captures this value for _Product & Engineering_ teams (Product decisions, Bug reports, Design reviews) which Gong ignores.
