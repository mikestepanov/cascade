# Competitor Analysis: Jibble

> **Focus:** Physical Accountability & Biometrics
> **Vibe:** "Construction/Field" - You _must_ be there.

## 1. Deep Dive: Jibble (The "Hard" Tracker)

**Why it wins:** Physical accountability. Prevents buddy punching in high-trust or hourly environments.
**Weakness:** Too aggressive for knowledge workers/devs. Can feel distrustful.

### "Scrappable" Features

- **Speed Mode:** Face recognition clock-in (biometric selfie).
- **Geofencing:** Can only clock in if GPS matches job site or IP matches office/VPN.
