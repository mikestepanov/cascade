# Competitor Analysis: Toggl Track

> **Focus:** Low Friction & Developer Friendly
> **Vibe:** "Developer/Freelancer" - Trusted reporting.

## 1. Deep Dive: Toggl (The "Friendly" Tracker)

**Why it wins:** Low friction. One-click timer. Strong Pomodoro integration.
**Weakness:** Expensive for teams ($18/user/mo). Reporting can be basic for enterprise.

### "Scrappable" Features

- **Timeline:** Visual bar chart of your day (Desktop App). Gaps are obvious to the user.
- **Integrations:** "Start Timer" button injected into Jira/GitHub/GitLab UI (Browser Extension).
