1. **Visual Appeal & Ease of Use**

---

## Weaknesses âŒ

1. **Cost & Complexity**
   - $19/user/month for "Pro" features (Time tracking, private boards) is very high.
   - Pricing tiers are confusing and strict limits on automation runs.

2. **Not "Developer Native"**
   - It feels like a spreadsheet turned into an app, not a dedicated issue tracker.
   - Keyboard shortcuts are weak compared to Linear/Jira.
   - UI is "cluttered" for minimalists.

3. **Performance**
   - Can get slow with thousands of items on a board.
   - Heavy client-side rendering.

4. **Mobile App**
   - Functional, but often criticized for being limited compared to desktop.
