# ClickUp - Competitor Analysis

> **Category:** All-in-One Productivity Platform
> **Type:** Proprietary SaaS
> **Website:** https://clickup.com

---

## Overview

**ClickUp** markets itself as the "One app to replace them all." It aggregates tasks, docs, chat, goals, and whiteboards into a single platform.

## Pricing

- **Free Forever:** 100MB storage, unlimited tasks.
- **Unlimited:** ~$7/user/mo (Unlimited storage, integrating, dashboards).
- **Business:** ~$12/user/mo (Google SSO, advanced time tracking).
- **Enterprise:** Custom.

## Features

- **Everything View:** See tasks across all spaces.
- **Highly Customizable:** 15+ views (List, Board, Calendar, Gantt, Box, etc.).
- **ClickUp Brain (AI):** AI integrated everywhere for summaries and writing.
- **Docs:** Built-in document editing (Notion-like).
- **Whiteboards:** Visual collaboration.

## Strengths ✅

- **Feature Density:** Has almost every feature imaginable.
- **Value:** "Unlimited" plan is very cheap ($7) compared to competitors.
- **Flexibility:** Can be shaped to fit any workflow.

## Weaknesses ❌

- **Complexity:** Steep learning curve; often feels "bloated" or overwhelming.
- **Performance:** Historically slower than Linear/Jira due to feature weight.
- **UX Clutter:** Too many buttons and options on screen.
